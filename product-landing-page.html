About us

Star Began its journey in 2019 as a single cake shop in Mumbai and
today we are growing enormously because of our quality products.
Today we are present in more than 12 cities across Mumbai, Navi-Mumbai &
Thane region. We endeavor to provide the family of our customers with
safe, appetizing nutritious and quality bakery products at reasonable prices.
We are initially aligned and externally focused to provide outstanding customer service
and believe customer satisfaction is our ultimate goal. Our Mission is to
bake happiness by serving the sweetest smiles to our customers and
this journey will continue as we open new outlets across Mumbai and other regions.
